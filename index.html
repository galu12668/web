SCHOOL BACKGROUND

Galu Primary School is located at Mwabungo village, Kinondo sub-location, Diani location, Msambweni sub-county in Kwale county. Te school was builtby the community in 1996 to bring education closer to their children and save them from walking long distances to school. It started in muddy structures with thatched makuti roof. The enrollment was 58 pupils with only one teacher. The first batch did itsCertificate of Primary Education examination in 1972. The academic performance results were impressive. A good number of the candidates were absorbed in government schools. In 1990 a donor named Verkaart came and transformed the school from temporary structures to permanent buildings. He built modern classrooms with brick walls, iron sheets, well ventilated windows and doors. In 1994 another donor came in and constructed a modern administration block, 6 toilets, and a school shade, financed electricity installation and bought 100 desks.

COCURRICULAR ACTIVITIES AND CLUBS

COCURRICULAR PROGRAMME

The school takes an active part in cocurricular activities up to national levels in music and county level in athletics

CLUBS AND SOCIETIES

We have functional clubs such as scouts and girlguides,debate,music,environmental,young farmers club as well as active societies which are christian union and young muslims. These societies help impacting learners with moral and spiritual values that guide them in their lives.
